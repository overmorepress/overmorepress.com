For Counselors, Therapists & Schools

Tools for the professionals already doing this work — bulk pricing for your practice, classroom, or program.

🍰

Built to Support Your Work, Not Replace It

Baked & Blended: All the Right Ingredients and A Recipe for Connection give blended families a shared, low-pressure language — a story to read together, and a structured way to keep talking between sessions. Both are designed to complement clinical or classroom work, not substitute for it.

Who These Resources Are For

School Counselors & Social Workers

A shelf-ready resource for students navigating two homes, and a card deck for individual or small-group check-ins.

Family Therapy Practices

Between-session tools that give families a structured way to continue building connection — particularly the deck's later cards, which surface material worth exploring in session.

Pediatric & Family Medicine Offices

A warm waiting-room or gift title for families in transition.

Family Court Programs & Guardian ad Litem Offices

A neutral, non-clinical resource to offer families navigating custody and co-parenting transitions.

Foster Care Agencies & Family Support Nonprofits

Accessible, non-therapy-speak language for families building new configurations.
