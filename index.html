Baked & Blended

All the Right Ingredients

What's the secret ingredient for family?

Jojo Bean thinks it might be candy, sprinkles, or frosting… but Bonus Mama says it's something even better.

When Jojo Bean worries about how to celebrate a birthday across two different homes, he discovers that love — like baking — rises slowly, sticks together, and finds its own way to be just right.

A warm and whimsical read-aloud for blended families, stepfamilies, and anyone who believes love comes in many flavors. The perfect gift for birthdays, holidays, and bedtime snuggles.

About the Author

Stacie Mizzi is an author and entrepreneur whose work is rooted in a lifelong passion for inspiring women and families to experience life to the fullest. A mother of three and proud stepmother, she writes for children navigating blended families and non-traditional family structures — with the belief that every child deserves to see their family reflected in the books they love.
