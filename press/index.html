About the Author

Stacie Mizzi

Author & Publisher, Overmore Press

Stacie Mizzi is an author and entrepreneur whose work is rooted in a lifelong passion for inspiring women and families to experience life to the fullest. A mother of three and proud stepmother, she was inspired to write her debut picture book, Baked & Blended: All the Right Ingredients, by her own blended family experience. She writes for children navigating blended families and non-traditional family structures with the belief that every child deserves to see their family reflected in the books they love. She publishes under Overmore Press.
