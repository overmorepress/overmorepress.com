Stacie Mizzi

Author & Founder, Overmore Press

Stacie Mizzi is an author and entrepreneur whose work is rooted in a lifelong passion for inspiring women and families to experience life to the fullest. A mother of three and proud stepmother, she was inspired to write her debut picture book, Baked & Blended: All the Right Ingredients, by her own blended family experience.

She writes for children navigating blended families and non-traditional family structures, with the belief that every child deserves to see their family reflected in the books they love.

🍰

Why "Overmore"

Overmore Press is a publishing imprint for women and families navigating seasons that don't have a lot of great language yet — blended family life, marriage, pregnancy, waiting, and rebuilding. Every title is written so that someone picks it up and finally feels seen.

Overmore Press launched with Baked & Blended, a children's series for blended families. The first book, All the Right Ingredients, is available now, along with the first set of conversation cards. Devotionals, journals, and resources for the women living these stories are forthcoming.

♥

Also Behind Abundance Brands

Overmore Press is published by Abundance Brands LLC, Stacie's Michigan-based marketing consultancy. If you're curious about that side of her work, you can find it at abundancebrands.com.
